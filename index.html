Rhythm Sketch

  

An easy way to jot down and edit musical notes.

Welcome to Rhythm Sketch.

Write down your musical compositions by placing graphical notes on a grid.

How to use:

  1. Click "Record," then press some keys from the top two keyboard rows. (` through Backspace, and Tab through \) to create objects on the grid in real time. Each object represents a musical note -- the higher on the grid, the higher the note's pitch.
  2. Click "Add notes," then click on the grid wherever you wish to place an additional note.
  3. Click "Edit notes," then click-and-drag the notes around to adjust the pitch and timing. You can also click on the right or left edge of a note, then drag to lengthen or shorten it.
  4. Click "Remove notes," then click on any note you wish to remove from the grid.
